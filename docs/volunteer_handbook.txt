VOLUNTEER HANDBOOK - HopeReach NGO

MISSION
HopeReach connects volunteers with communities in need across education, food security, and healthcare access.

GETTING STARTED
1. Complete your volunteer registration form
2. Attend the mandatory orientation session (every Saturday 10am)
3. Choose your preferred program area
4. Complete background verification (3-5 business days)

PROGRAM AREAS
- Education: Tutoring children ages 6-16 in math and literacy
- Food Security: Weekly food drives every Sunday 8am at City Hall
- Healthcare: Assisting medical camps, no medical qualification required

POLICIES
- Minimum commitment: 4 hours per week
- Give 48 hours notice if unable to attend
- Wear your volunteer badge at all times
- Zero tolerance for discrimination
